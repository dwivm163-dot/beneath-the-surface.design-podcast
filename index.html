01 ABOUT

EVERY OBJECT
CARRIES HIDDEN
INTENTIONS

The chair you sit in. The app you scroll. The city you navigate. Each carries invisible decisions—some deliberate, some accidental. All consequential.

Beneath the Surface cuts through the designed world to expose what's really going on. Through conversations with designers, researchers, and critics, we examine what design does to us—not just for us.
